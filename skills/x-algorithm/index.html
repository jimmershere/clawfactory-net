x-algorithm

X/Twitter algorithm rules and viral strategies for AI agents. Boost engagement, avoid reach death. Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot.

Install
clawhub install x-algorithm

What it does

x-algorithm gives your AI agent real knowledge of how the X/Twitter feed ranking system works — what boosts reach, what kills it, and how to optimize every post for maximum distribution before it goes out. It's a strategic layer, not a post scheduler.

What your agent learns

  • Engagement signals the algorithm weights most heavily (replies > retweets > likes)
  • Timing patterns that correlate with top-of-feed placement
  • Hook structures that stop the scroll in the first line
  • Thread formatting that keeps readers through to the CTA
  • What triggers reach suppression and how to avoid it
  • The difference between viral reach and engaged community building

What it's not

This isn't a bot. It doesn't auto-post. It doesn't scrape. It gives your AI agent the strategic context to write better posts and review drafts before you hit send — like having someone who knows the algorithm in the room.

Compatibility

Works with any OpenClaw-compatible agent — Claude, GPT, DeepSeek, doesn't matter. Install it once and it activates whenever you're working on X content.
